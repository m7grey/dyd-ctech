Chris Fox. CEO at Mighty Schools.

In 2015, Anna decided to open up a dance studio in New York, focusing on Sensual Bachata, which she is very passionate about. After attending multiple workshops from her idols – Daniel & Desiree, Sensual Bachata was the style she wanted to pursue and bring that soft touch to New York to share her passion with the students here. Anna cofounded a dance studio in December 2015 – Sensual Movement Studio, with the team representing the studio called New York Sensual Movement. Her goal is to take Bachata to a whole new level – very classy, elegant and sensual, which is still yet to be developed in New York!
